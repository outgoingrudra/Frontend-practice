Emma Myers — Rising Star of Hollywood

Known for her breakout role in Wednesday, Emma continues to win hearts with her charm and talent.

Emma Myers is an American actress best known for her breakout role as Enid Sinclair in Netflix’s hit series Wednesday. Born in 2002, she began her career in minor roles before gaining international recognition. With her charismatic screen presence and growing popularity, Emma is quickly becoming one of Hollywood’s most promising young talents, admired by fans worldwide.
